About Me

Hello, I'm Rex!

I'm currently a second year student at the University of British Columbia studying Computer Science and Physics. I love using logic to break down and solve problems, whether they are physics, math, computer science, or even other subjects, like psychology, biology, or economics.

In my spare time I self-taught myself Java. I've used this knowledge when I've encountered various problems, creating my own solutions rather than succumbing to them.

I absolutely love learning new things, and programming has become an expressive tool for me, where with some time and dedication (and a bit of StackOverflow), I can create anything I can imagine.

Currently, I am continuing development of my Tower Defense Game, TRACV. I am also trying to learn Python and JavaScript through some smaller projects.
